About the Fest

The Art & Culture Fest 2025 is a celebration of creativity, diversity, and heritage. Join us for a 3-day extravaganza filled with music, dance, art exhibits, cultural performances, workshops, and more!

Event Highlights

Live Performances

From classical dances to indie bands, enjoy spectacular performances by renowned artists.

Workshops & Exhibitions

Participate in hands-on workshops and explore art exhibits showcasing local talent.

Food & Crafts Market

Experience the tastes and traditions of different cultures through our curated stalls.

Open Mic & Street Art

Unleash your talent or enjoy spontaneous creativity in our vibrant street zone.
